    The CloudSOCTM Platform

    The Elastica CloudSOC platform enables companies to confidently
    leverage cloud applications and services while staying safe, secure
    and compliant. Leveraging advanced data science and machine
    learning, CloudSOC taps real-time user traffic, native SaaS APIs and
    other data sources to provide a single pane of glass for monitoring and
    controlling your SaaS apps.

    Elastica apps

    Elastica Security Apps are deployed on the extensible CloudSOC platform and
    deliver the full security life cycle across multiple cloud apps.

    Elastica SecurletsTM

    Elastica Securlets are standalone security applications on the
    CloudSOC platform that provide advanced security functionality for a
    specific SaaS application. They can be deployed on their own or in
    combination with any of the Elastica Apps.
